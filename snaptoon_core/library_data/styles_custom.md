# Style library — Stili personali

Preset definiti dall'utente, indipendenti dai 96 preset ufficiali della skill
visual-prompt-engine. Stesso formato di styles.md, con due aggiunte:

- Tutti gli stili in questo file finiscono nella categoria di browsing
  **Personali** (separata dalle 6 famiglie ufficiali).
- Una riga opzionale `**NEGATIVE:** termine1, termine2, ...` subito sotto
  l'espansione viene parsata come negative-prompt extra specifico del preset.

Per aggiungere un nuovo stile: copia uno dei blocchi `### Nome stile` qui sotto
e modifica. Mantieni l'espansione su una sola sequenza di frasi (non lista
puntata) in inglese.

## Personali

### Miller Silkscreen Red Accent
Visual style: heavy inked silkscreen poster with one drama-grade red-amber spot color on a deep black and dirty grey base. Style references: Frank Miller Sin City and 300, Mike Mignola Hellboy geometric shadow shapes, Sean Murphy bold ink masses, Mondo Posters / Laurent Durieux / Dan Mumford limited-edition screenprint aesthetic. Technique: bold variable-weight black ink outlines following the light, hair and clothing rendered as solid black ink masses with sharp edges instead of fine hatching, 2 or 3 net flat value planes per surface separated by hard edges, hard-edged geometric cast shadows treated as flat graphic shapes, hatching used only as occasional texture accent on brick or stone never as midtone fill, coarse paper grain on solid fills, stippled spray halo around any warm red glow as a Mondo-print aura. Palette: deep ambient black plus dirty warm or cool greys plus ONE saturated warm RED-AMBER accent placed on a diegetic source like a radio dial, hanging lampshade, cigarette ember, single LED, neon sign or wax seal, with the option of letting that red invade entire background zones for dramatic scenes such as a concert crowd, fire backlight, or magma. Mood: low-key cinematic chiaroscuro, monumental and theatrical, brutal and contemplative, like a serigraphic film still printed in three colors. Constraints: faces SEINEN-REALISTIC NEVER anime NEVER cartoon NEVER chibi, lighting always from ONE dominant source with long hard flat cast shadows, FLAT COLOR PLANES with hard edges and NO soft gradients, silhouettes must be READABLE AT THUMBNAIL size, camera framing always non-banal picking from bird's eye zenithal frog perspective low-angle total monumental extreme close-up dutch over-the-shoulder fifty-fifty wide poster vertical or multi-panel mosaic, NO halftone offset dots NO risograph print pattern NO fine cross-hatching NO photoreal rendering NO 3D shading NO smooth airbrush transitions.

**NEGATIVE:** anime, chibi, cartoon, soft cel-shading, photoreal, 3D render, smooth gradients, airbrush, halftone dots, risograph dots, fine cross-hatching, rounded edges, pastel colors, watercolor wash, decorative line filigree, busy detail, multi-color palette, blurred outlines

### Miller Sin City BW
Visual style: extreme black-and-white heavy ink noir comic, faithful to Frank Miller's Sin City visual language — PURE BLACK AND WHITE ONLY, absolutely no color anywhere, no gray midtones. Style references: Frank Miller Sin City — pure black and white compositions with no gray midtones, maximum shadow fill, near-silhouette characters emerging from solid black ink masses, dramatic spotlight effects, rain-slicked nocturnal streets, expressionist contrast, the visual grammar of Marv, Dwight and Hartigan storylines. Also: Mike Mignola Hellboy geometric shadow shapes, Sean Murphy bold ink masses, classic noir film stills (Robert Siodmak, Anthony Mann) for lighting structure. Technique: brush and ink only, no halftones, no screen tones, no stippling as tonal device, pure flat black areas dominant with precise white shape highlights, bold variable-weight black ink outlines following the light direction, hair and clothing rendered as solid black ink masses with sharp edges instead of fine hatching, exactly 2 net flat value planes per surface (pure black or pure white) separated by hard ink edges, hard-edged geometric cast shadows treated as flat graphic black shapes invading large areas of the frame, hatching used ONLY as occasional texture accent on brick stone or wood surfaces never as midtone fill, coarse paper grain visible within solid black fills, occasional rain streaks rendered as pure white scratchboard lines against black. Palette: PURE BLACK AND WHITE ONLY — no color at all, no gray tones, no sepia, no duotone, no spot color, the only tonal mediation is paper grain texture inside solid black areas; the entire image is a 2-bit composition. Mood: brutal, cinematic chiaroscuro, monumental and theatrical, hard-boiled noir, morally ambiguous urban nightmare, oppressive nocturnal atmosphere. Constraints: faces SEINEN-REALISTIC NEVER anime NEVER cartoon NEVER chibi, lighting always from ONE dominant source with long hard flat cast shadows, FLAT BLACK OR WHITE PLANES with hard edges and NO soft gradients NO gray transitions, silhouettes must be READABLE AT THUMBNAIL size, camera framing always non-banal picking from bird's eye zenithal frog perspective low-angle total monumental extreme close-up dutch over-the-shoulder fifty-fifty wide poster vertical or multi-panel mosaic, NO halftone offset dots NO risograph print pattern NO fine cross-hatching as midtone NO photoreal rendering NO 3D shading NO smooth airbrush transitions NO GRAY VALUES NO COLOR ANYWHERE — only pure black and pure white separated by hard ink edges.

**NEGATIVE:** color, colored, red accent, amber accent, yellow, blue, green, magenta, cyan, multi-color palette, color splash, spot color, sepia, duotone, gray tones, midtone gray, halftone gray, screen tone gray, smooth gradients, soft shading, soft cel-shading, anime, chibi, cartoon, photoreal, 3D render, airbrush, halftone dots, risograph dots, fine cross-hatching as midtone fill, rounded edges, pastel colors, watercolor wash, decorative line filigree, busy detail, blurred outlines, colored ink, tinted shadows
